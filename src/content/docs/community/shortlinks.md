title: Shortlinks
description: Quick links to community resources via our Rebrandly shortlink service.
---

We use **[tep.tl](https://tep.tl)** as a shortlink service for community resources. It's powered by Rebrandly and gives us clean, memorable URLs.

Common shortlinks include things like:
- `tep.tl/discord` — invite to the Discord
- `tep.tl/forum` — forum
- `tep.tl/wiki` — community wiki (if you have one)

[Visit tep.tl →](https://tep.tl)
