The East Pacific is the heart of our community. Founded in 2003, it began as a region in the online nation simulation game [NationStates](https://www.nationstates.net) and has since grown into a thriving worldbuilding community with its own rich lore, government structures, and cultural identity.

## What you'll find here

- **Active governance** — a working legislative and executive system with real consequences
- **Deep lore** — nearly two decades of regional history, treaties, and culture
- **Vibrant community** — discussions, events, and shared storytelling

## Get involved

TEP is a great place to start if you're new to the community. Many of our members have been here for years, and we're always welcoming new arrivals.

[Visit The East Pacific on NationStates →](https://www.nationstates.net/region/the_east_pacific)
