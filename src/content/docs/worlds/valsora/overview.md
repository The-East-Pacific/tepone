Valsora is a long-form collaborative roleplay that takes place on the [TEP Regional Message Board](https://forum.theeastpacific.com) on NationStates. It's woven together one post at a time by the community itself.

## What you'll find here

- **Collaborative storytelling** — every post is a chapter
- **Rich character work** — deep character development across many authors
- **Living history** — the world changes as the story unfolds

## Get involved

Valsora welcomes new contributors. You can read along, or jump in and add to the story.
